Process

Step 1

Brief & consultation

We talk, I listen and together we define the project goals -- stye prefernces, site conditions, budget and timeline.

Step 2

Schematic Design

Rough sketches and basic floor plans to present the initial concept idea.

Step 3

Design development

We refine 3D model together, pick materials and finalize the look and feels;

Step 4

Construction documentation

Producing the full technical drawisng set -- the instruction manual for builders covering every detail of the contruction.

Step 5

Project handover

you receive the complete digital portofolio of your future home -- all files, renders and documentation ready for construction.

Revisions

Up to 3 revision rounds

Included across the full design process.

Services
